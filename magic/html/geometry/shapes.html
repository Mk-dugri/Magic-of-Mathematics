Shapes and Properties

Triangle

A triangle has three sides and three angles. The sum of its angles is always 180 degrees.

Square

A square has four equal sides and four right angles. Its diagonals bisect each other at right angles.

Rectangle

A rectangle has opposite sides that are equal and four right angles. Its diagonals are equal in length.

Circle

A circle is a set of points that are equidistant from a center point. The distance from the center to the circumference is called the radius.

Pentagon

A pentagon has five sides and five angles. The sum of its internal angles is 540 degrees.

Hexagon

A hexagon has six sides and six angles. The sum of its internal angles is 720 degrees.
