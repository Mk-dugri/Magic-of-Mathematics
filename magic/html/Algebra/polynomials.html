Polynomials

Introduction to Polynomials

Polynomials are algebraic expressions that consist of variables and coefficients, involving only the operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication, and non-negative integer exponents. Example:

P(x) = 4x3 + 3x2 - 2x + 7

Types of Polynomials

Understand the different types of polynomials:

  • Monomials: \(5x^3\)
  • Binomials: \(3x^2 - 2x\)
  • Trinomials: \(x^2 + 4x + 4\)

Polynomial Operations

Learn how to perform operations on polynomials, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Factoring Polynomials

Explore different methods of factoring polynomials such as:

  • Factoring by grouping
  • Using the quadratic formula
  • Factoring special products

Polynomial Equations

Understand how to solve polynomial equations by setting the polynomial equal to zero and finding the roots.

Applications of Polynomials

Polynomials are used in various real-world applications including physics, engineering, economics, and more.
